DISCOVER THE WONDER OF ROXAS CITY CAPIZ


Capiz Provincial Park


Capiz Provincial Park is a tranquil landmark nestled in the heart of Roxas City, Philippines. It’s a place where nature and history intertwine, featuring verdant landscapes, pocket gardens, and a charming lagoon with a rocky fountain centerpiece. This park serves as a peaceful sanctuary for those seeking to unwind amidst the beauty of native trees and plants endemic to the Philippines. It’s a testament to the region’s commitment to preserving natural beauty and local culture.
